Giant Plumose Anemone

Giant Plumose Anemone is a type of species of Sea Anemone. It is found in the very deep waters along the coast if the United States and Canada.

GPA's grow to up tp 3 ft, they're white as seen in the picture to the left and they have a furry Pouf on top. Although they may look like harmless plants, they sting and catch prey with their tentacles.

Lotus Flower

Their existence stretches back some 145.5 million years. Lotus flowers even survived the Ice Age (1.8 million – 10,000 years ago). Most plants in the northern hemisphere became extinct during this era.

Lotus flowers appear most prominently in wetlands and usually grow in mud. But they are also very adaptive to their local environments. They can survive under ice as long as their roots remain in water or mud.

The lotus has a unique daily life cycle of life, death, and rebirth. This has led to the phrase, “lotus flower of life”.That is why it’s meaning is often “rebirth”, and why the flower is frequently tied to spirituality.
